Member benefits

By becoming a member of Women in Medicine, you will become a part of a support community. You will be able to attend company gatherings that include guest speakers and social events!

Qualifications

In order to become a member, you must be living in the Pittsburgh area. In addition, you must be either a medical student or professional a part of the Allegheny Health Network.
